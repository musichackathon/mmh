French guitarist Didier Aschour performs “Buzzing,” an exploration of the unusual chords that emerge when using an e-bow and slide on a steel string acoustic guitar. Performed at Monthly Music Hackathon NYC August 25th, 2012, hosted by exfm (http://ex.fm).
