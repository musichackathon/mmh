At the February 2013 Music Hackathon NYC, Jamie Burkart made a Max/MSP program that breaks up audio into small segments and sorts them based on some feature of the clips. He says:

“Ooops, I broke Spencer Owen’s song Jeu des Treize (spencerowen.bandcamp.com/track/jeu-des-treize) into tiny pieces. I tried to put it back together, sorting the pieces by pitch. Here you go!”
