We’re taking a short break from Monthly Music Hackathons. We’ll be back in June 2017 with Hip Hop Hacks!

Coming up later in 2017, we’ll have music hackathons exploring Music Education, Composition/Performance/Instruments, the Science of Music, Music Accessibility, Music Visualization, and more! At every event we open the day with inspiring talks; have group brainstorming sessions; hands-on music, tech, and creativity workshops; an afternoon to work on a project in a group or on your own, and demos and performances of what you made in the evening. Our events are free, open to everyone, and all are welcome! People at every skill level in any discipline are invited to come collaborate and learn from each other. Please join us in June!
